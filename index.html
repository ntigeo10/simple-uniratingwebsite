Welcome!

Welcome to University Rating System based on SDGs. Here, you can rate any university you want and get the final results of is rate of the year. This service can also help you compare the results based on previous years, specifically the reference year of 2022. Those comparisons will help you understand in which SDGs should your university improve or in which SDGs your university has deteriorated. If you need more help with the service, please check the About page, where you will find more info about the project and the service and about the creator of this site.
